ABOUT

We have a direct and personal contact with the main players in the industry which are the mines. we have a depot at Ha-Mabote which has the capacity to hold in excess of 1000 tons of coal.We pride ourselves in delivering outstanding service to both small and large coal users. We are committed to providing excellent service and endeavors to further improving score. We have a dedicated sales team with years of experience under their belt. We strive to ensure that the client receives our best attention and effort at all times. We own a fleet of trucks enabling us to supply our customers with unlimited notice, 365 days a year. Nevertheless, in the case of bulk, the majority of our products is moved directly from the mines to the customer in order to reduce handling costs.
